# Super-Store-Sales-Power-BI-Dashboard-Creation-Project

Project Overview - Super Store is a small retail business located in the United States. They sell Furniture, Office Supplies and Technology products and their customers are the Consumer, Corporate and Home Offices. The data set contains sales, profit and geographical information of Super Store.

The goal of project is to analyse the sales data, identify demanded areas and low sales growth areas to create opportunities for Super Store to boost business growth, and to understand the sales by category, sub-category and to check yoy growth Super Stores .
